My favorite guitarists to listen to & learn from!

Ever since I heard my first Metallica album (Master of Puppets), I've always wanted to learn how to play electric guitar just like Kirk Hammett and James Hetfield. Over the years, my repertoire and taste in music evolved to not only include heavy rock, but flamenco guitar as well. I want to share with everyone a few of my greatest inspirations for learning how to play guitar, and I want to encourage everyone to always have an open mind in listening to different styles of music. You would be surprised what you can come to love!
